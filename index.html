About Me

After receiving my PhD in computational neuroscience from MIT in 2008, I co-founded two companies based on probabilistic inference; both were backed by the crazy folks at Founders Fund. The second, Prior Knowledge, was acquired by Salesforce in late 2012, where I now work as a product manager.

I live in Oakland, CA with my wife and daughter.
